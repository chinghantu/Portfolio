User Research

In order to better understand the needs of the patients, we had attended three events and conducted research on patients and caregivers with different symptoms and under different conditions. In total, we had interviewed 10 patients and caregivers.

I had the opportunity to visit one of the patients and caregivers house where I was able to have a deeper conversation and understand better their daily lives.

Problems

Level One
Forget to eat pills on time
Patients without monitoring are at higher risk of accidents
Visuospatial cognition degeneration

Level Two
Patients forget to eat pills on time
Patients forget they had eaten the pills
Setting up time interval for different day is troublesome

Empathy Map & As-Is Scenario Map
Empathy Map is separated into four sections:
Top Left: What patients say
Top Right: What patients think
Bottom Left: What patients does
Bottom Right: What patients feels
As-Is Scenario Map is when we organized what we put on in empathy maps in the order of steps, does, thinks, and feels.

Then each member of the group voted on the problems that we think the most important.

Solution

A synced system using several devices to remind patient and caregiver of prescription time, with an app, a pill box, and a bracelet.
